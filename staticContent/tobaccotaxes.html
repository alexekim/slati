High taxes on tobacco products are a proven way to reduce tobacco use especially among youth. The current federal cigarette tax is $1.01 per pack, and the current average state cigarette tax is $1.72 per pack. Three states, Connecticut, Delaware and Rhode Island, have increased their cigarette tax in 2017.
